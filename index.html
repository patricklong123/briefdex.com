The product

Five channels. One morning routine.

Briefdex delivers five daily audio briefings covering everything that moves New Zealand markets. All channels included with your subscription.

Daily Wrap
The one thing that moved markets overnight, a finance concept taught through today's news, and what it means for your money. 7 minutes. The complete picture.
NZ Markets
The NZX session in full — stocks-first, what moved and why, and what it means if you own them directly or hold them in KiwiSaver. ~5 minutes.
Global Markets
Wall Street, the Fed, oil, gold — told through the stocks that actually moved, not just the index. Rogue movers included. ~4–6 minutes.
Trans-Tasman
The ASX and RBA through a Kiwi lens — your travel money, your bank's parent company, your KiwiSaver's Australian sleeve. ~3 minutes.
Macro & RBNZ
OCR decisions, inflation, Stats NZ data — and what the Reserve Bank is watching before its next move. ~3–4 minutes.
How it works

From primary source to your ears in hours.

Every briefing runs through the same overnight pipeline. Sources in. Audio out. Ready before you wake up.

1
Official sources
NZX and ASX announcements, RBNZ and RBA releases, Stats NZ data, Beehive releases and live market feeds — pulled directly from the source, every night.
2
Directed and written
A human sets the editorial direction on our New Zealand and Trans-Tasman coverage each day — what leads, what angle matters for your wallet. The script is written from those verified sources, and every claim is checked against the source data before it's published. Every piece of jargon defined in plain English.
3
Audio delivered
Your briefing is ready by 6:30am — before the market opens — and stays in the app all day. Listen on your commute, your walk, or whenever suits.
4
Walk in informed
Seven minutes later you know what moved overnight, what the RBNZ is watching, and what to track today.

AI-scripted and voiced. Global markets coverage is fully automated.
