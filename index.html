The product

Five channels. One morning routine.

Briefdex delivers five daily audio briefings covering everything that moves New Zealand markets. All channels included with your subscription.

Daily Wrap
The one thing that moved markets overnight, a finance concept taught through today's news, and what it means for your money. 7 minutes. The complete picture.
NZ Markets
The NZX session in full — stocks-first, what moved and why, and what it means if you hold them on Sharesies or in KiwiSaver.
Global Markets
Wall Street, the Fed, oil, gold — told through the stocks that actually moved, not just the index. Rogue movers included.
Trans-Tasman
The ASX and RBA through a Kiwi lens — your travel money, your bank's parent company, your KiwiSaver's Australian sleeve.
Macro & RBNZ
OCR decisions, inflation, Stats NZ data — and what the Reserve Bank is watching before its next move.
How it works

From primary source to your ears in hours.

Every briefing runs through the same overnight pipeline. Sources in. Audio out. Ready before you wake up.

1
Primary sources
NZX announcements, RBNZ releases, Stats NZ data, Beehive press releases — pulled directly from the source, every night.
2
Script written
AI generates the script from verified primary sources. Every number confirmed. Every piece of jargon defined in plain English.
3
Audio delivered
Your briefing is ready by 6:30am — before the market opens. Listen on your commute, your walk, or at your desk.
4
Walk in informed
Seven minutes later you know what moved overnight, what the RBNZ is watching, and what to track today.
